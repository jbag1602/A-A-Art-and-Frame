Directions




201 West Center

Provo, Utah 84601

200 West is also called Freedom Boulevard.

Take the East Bound Provo Center Street Exit #265. Head east until you reach Freedom Boulevard, which is 200 West.

We are the tan building on the south west corner. The Convention Center is across the street to the north and Bingham's Cyclery is across the street on the east. We are right next door to Telisa's.

Two hour parking is available for you on both sides of Center Street. If you need free long term parking you can park in the parking lot just North of the Convention Center. Due to all the construction in Downtown Provo, parking is changing. There are signs which show public parking.
