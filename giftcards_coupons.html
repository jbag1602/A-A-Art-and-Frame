Gift Cards




Gift cards can be a great gift for your family and friends. You can purchase our gift cards in any dollar amount and pay by cash, card, or check. They can be used towards fine art supplies, custom framing, easel rentals, books, drafting supplies, specialty papers, matcutters, ready made frames and mats, and more!


Would you like to purchase gift cards as an award for your event? We can do that too.


Would you like a gift card for an art class or workshop? You can either pay by cash or check and we will give you a gift card for your selected art class.


Call our customer service at 801-375-1150 to purchase a gift card by credit card or come into the store. If you would like us to mail a gift card to you or another person please let us know.
