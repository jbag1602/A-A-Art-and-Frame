Gessoed Masonite Panels

Artist Quality Gessoed Art Board is an ideal surface for painting with oils, acrylics and watercolors, or drawing with pencils, pastels, chalk, or pen & ink. This is the perfect choice of surface for Rub Out painting technique, and is excellent for texture painting as well. Gessoed Art Board are smooth faced, double primed, acid free and ready for your art projects. They withstand atmospheric conditions better than canvas products, and are easy to frame.
