READY MADE MAT SIZES




Our Standard Sizes:

5 x 7 outside with a 3 x 4.5 window
8 x 10 outside with a 4.5 x 6.5 window
9 x 12 outside with a 5.5 x 8.5 window
11 x 14 outside with a 7.5 x 9.5 window
16 x 20 outside with a 7.5 x 9.5 window
16 x 20 outside with a 10.5 x 13.5 window
16 x 20 outside with a 11.5 x 15.5 window


The mats are available in single and double mats and are cut to fit these standard size photos: 3.5 x 5, 5 x 7, 8 x 10, 11 x 14 and 12 x 16. We also offer a few multi-opening mats and mats that include fillets.

If you need a mat for a wedding reception where your guests can sign the mat a common size is the 16 x 20 mat which will fit an 8 x 10 photo. You will want a few nice pens for your guests to use and we recommend the black, gold or silver gel pens. They do not bleed and are acid free.
